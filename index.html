Welcome to Our Institute

The SBS Foundation (Promoted by Jeevan Shiksha Manadal Samiti), duly Registered Under the Provisions of Madyha Pradesh Societies Registration Act,1973 on -30/04/1983 day of year 1983 The prime objective of the Society is to provide facilities for quality education to boys and girls of all ages by establishing-
• Schools to offer primary and secondary education
• Professional institutions to offer Communication, Management and Computer Sciences courses at all levels, i.e. graduate , post-graduate, doctoral and post-doctoral, and Research and Consultancy Services
